Tembea Africa

Tembea africa national park (TANP) is located south of Nairobi CBD in langata at the Kenya Wildlife Sercice HQ or KWS. We have the safari walk (1st right as you drive in) comprising of large open enclosures for the larger animals ie zebra, hyena, Rhino, lions etc as well as sealed off ones for monkeys and leopards. It's a fun activity especially if you have children, just mind the noise. If you're lucky you might see them being fed at around 4pm.
